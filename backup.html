ThinkArt

ThinkArt is a flutter application based on selling of art/paintings in the digital market. In the current market , due to COVID 19, many artists have lost their jobs and also all public art galleries and public auction of paintings are closed. This is where ThinkArt comes in. This application has everything a customer wants when going to look for buying a painting.
